Areas

Human-AI Interaction, Accessibility, Tools For Thoughts

I contribute interactive systems empowered by models and algorithms to address real world problems, and elicit user insights throughout the design process and evaluation.

Most of my work is related to accessibility, as I believe AI should enable people, regardless of their abilities.

My recent work at Google Deepmind focuses on tools for thoughts, where we imagine a world where AI can help users think.
